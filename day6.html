Day 6
Dear Sappy,

We went to Genting Highlands today. I love her. I realized that today.
I really do. Watching her laugh and be happy, I realized that I need her in my life.
I want to be the one of the reasons why she laughs and gets all red with excitement.
I thought it was just a crush. I really did.
I was wrong.
I love her.
I want her.
I whispered all that to her during the rides. When we were falling from the top. Well, she was, I was only falling in love with her with every second. God please let me tell her. Let there be a a miracle. Let me live my life the way I want it. At least this one time.

Update: There she was on my bed. I was asked who I liked. I said a girl who wears blue.
I swear I heard the words "I like you". And so we found love like morning dew.

Sincerely,
Hers.
